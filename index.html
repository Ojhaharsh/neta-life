About NetaLife

Why This Matters

🗳️

Post-Election Accountability

Pre-election declarations are just the beginning. We track what happens AFTER they win — attendance, questions raised, debates, fund utilization. Real accountability, not just promises on paper.

📊

Data-Driven

Every metric is sourced from PRS Legislative Research (prsindia.org) — India's most trusted independent parliamentary watchdog since 2005.

🤝

Community Powered

Citizens can contribute evidence, flag discrepancies, and help verify promise delivery status. Democracy is a team sport.

🔓

Open & Non-Partisan

No political affiliation. No bias. Just data. All our methodology is transparent and open to scrutiny.
